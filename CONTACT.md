# Contact & Support

If you have questions or need support with PDFToolskit, here's how to reach us:

## Technical Support
- **Issues**: Report bugs or request features via our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/PDFToolskit/pdftoolskit/issues).
- **Discussions**: For general questions, please use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/PDFToolskit/pdftoolskit/discussions).

## Institutional / Enterprise
For enterprise support, licensing questions, or institutional deployment, please contact:
- **Email**: support@pdftoolskit.io
- **Website**: [pdftoolskit.io/enterprise](https://pdftoolskit.io/enterprise)

## Security
To report security vulnerabilities, please do NOT open a public issue. Email us directly at security@pdftoolskit.io.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours.
